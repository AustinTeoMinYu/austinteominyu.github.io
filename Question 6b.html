Exclusive Recipe To Cook Chicken Curry

Ingrediants
  • 800g Chicken
  • 1/2-1 coconut milks
  • 180g curry paste
  • 3 Large Potato
  • 3 Large Onion
  • 3 Stalks Curry Leaves
Cooking Steps
  1. Saute curry paste in hot oil until fragrant.
  2. Add in chicken.Continue to saute until chicken releases oil.
  3. Add in water, tamarind paste, curry leaves, potato, onion, sugar and salt.
  4. Let chicken simmer in slow fire until potatoes become soft and chicken isc cooked throughly.
  5. Lastly, add in coconut milk and cook until oil starts to seperate.Remove and serve
